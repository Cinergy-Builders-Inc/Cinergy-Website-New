BEFORE / AFTER PHOTO SETUP

The homepage is ready for 3 before-and-after sliders.

Just place your photos in the images folder using these filenames:

before-1.jpg
after-1.jpg

before-2.jpg
after-2.jpg

before-3.jpg
after-3.jpg

Then refresh the site.

You do not need to change any code unless you want to add more than 3 sliders.
