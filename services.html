24/7 Catastrophic Response

Rapid response when widespread damage demands immediate action.

Catastrophic events can leave homes and commercial properties exposed, unsafe, and difficult to stabilize without a coordinated plan. Cinergy Restoration responds quickly with emergency support built around safety, damage control, and a faster path toward recovery.

From severe storms and hurricanes to large-loss events that require debris removal, water extraction, structural assessment, and mold-related follow-up, our team works to restore order and protect the property while the full restoration plan takes shape.

  • Storm and large-loss response
  • Damage stabilization and assessment
  • Coordinated restoration support
Water Damage Restoration

Fast action for leaks, floods, plumbing failures, and storm-related water intrusion.

When water damage strikes, every hour matters. Cinergy Restoration provides prompt water damage restoration built around fast extraction, water removal, drying, cleanup, and repair so the problem does not continue to spread or create additional issues like mold or structural instability.

Whether the source is a burst pipe, appliance overflow, roof leak, or severe weather, our goal is to move quickly, clean thoroughly, and help return the property to a safe and stable condition with one coordinated process from emergency response through repair.

  • Water extraction and removal
  • Dry-out and cleanup support
  • Repair and restoration coordination
Fire Damage Restoration

Cleanup, repair, and rebuilding support after fire and smoke damage.

Fire damage can leave a property overwhelmed by soot, smoke, structural damage, and a long list of urgent decisions. Cinergy Restoration provides fire damage restoration services that help clients move forward with a clearer plan, professional communication, and reliable support through cleanup and recovery.

Our process is designed to address the visible damage and the deeper restoration needs that follow, so the property can be repaired thoroughly and brought back in a condition that feels safe, functional, and well handled.

  • Fire, smoke, and soot cleanup
  • Damage removal and restoration prep
  • Repair and rebuild coordination
Mold Remediation

Safe, professional remediation focused on restoring a healthier indoor environment.

Mold is not only a property issue. It can also create serious concerns for health, indoor air quality, and long-term damage if it is handled improperly. Cinergy Restoration takes a thorough, containment-minded approach to mold remediation so affected areas can be addressed without unnecessarily spreading spores through the space.

Our team works to identify the problem, remove compromised materials when needed, and help guide the property back toward a cleaner and more stable condition with practical communication every step of the way.

  • Containment-focused remediation
  • Health and safety awareness
  • Cleaner indoor recovery process
Reconstruction & Renovation

In-house rebuild services that create better continuity from damage control to final completion.

One of the biggest advantages of working with Cinergy Restoration is being able to move from mitigation into reconstruction without handing the project off to a completely separate contractor. That creates a smoother experience, stronger continuity, and fewer communication gaps for the client.

From structural repairs to finish work, our reconstruction and renovation services are designed to bring the property back together efficiently while maintaining the same service-first approach from start to finish.

  • Repair and renovation support
  • One team from mitigation to rebuild
  • Stronger project continuity
Roof Tarp Services

Temporary roof protection that helps reduce further damage after storms or sudden roof failure.

Roof damage can expose a property to water intrusion, weather, pests, and additional structural problems if it is not protected quickly. Cinergy Restoration provides emergency roof tarp services designed to secure the property and reduce the risk of further loss while the next phase of repair is being planned.

We respond with urgency, use durable materials, and focus on protecting the home or building as quickly as possible so the situation does not become more costly and more disruptive than it already is.

  • Emergency roof protection
  • Storm-ready temporary coverage
  • Helps reduce additional exposure
Biohazard Cleanup

Careful cleanup for environments that require a more controlled and safety-focused response.

Some loss situations require more than basic demolition or general cleanup. Biohazard cleanup calls for controlled handling, proper protective measures, and a process that prioritizes safety, discretion, and thoroughness.

Cinergy Restoration approaches these situations with professionalism and care so the affected area can be addressed responsibly and prepared for the next phase of recovery.

  • Safety-first cleanup procedures
  • Controlled response and handling
  • Professional and discreet support
Board Up Services

Fast property protection after storm damage, break-ins, fire, or major exposure.

When windows, doors, or openings are compromised, immediate board-up service helps secure the property and reduce the chance of additional damage or unwanted access. Cinergy Restoration provides rapid board-up support as part of a broader emergency-response approach.

That means the property can be protected quickly while restoration planning, cleanup, and next-step coordination continue in a more controlled way.

  • Emergency property security
  • Quick protective measures
  • Supports safer stabilization
Packout Services

Organized handling of belongings when the property needs deeper restoration work.

When damage affects large portions of the home or building, packout services can make the restoration process more manageable by clearing items out of the work area in a more organized way. This helps create room for cleanup, drying, repairs, and reconstruction while reducing disruption.

Cinergy Restoration integrates packout services into the overall restoration process so clients have one more layer of coordination and support during a stressful time.

  • More organized restoration workflow
  • Helps clear the work area
  • Supports larger cleanup and rebuild projects
