About Us

A restoration company built for responsive service and complete follow-through.

We combine emergency response, remediation, and reconstruction under one roof so clients can move through the process with less confusion and fewer delays.

In-house support from mitigation through rebuild.

Cinergy Restoration serves residential and commercial clients throughout South Florida with a practical, service-driven approach. Our work is designed to protect the property quickly, communicate clearly, and restore the space the right way.

We understand that damage to a home or business can be disruptive and stressful. That is why our team focuses on fast response times, professional coordination, and quality workmanship from the first call through the final phase of reconstruction.
